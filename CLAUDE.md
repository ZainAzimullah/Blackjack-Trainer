# CLAUDE.md — Blackjack Project

## Overview

This project implements a Blackjack simulator/engine. All game logic, strategy calculations, and simulations must conform exactly to the rule set defined below. Do not apply generic or "default" casino rules — this specific variant must be enforced throughout.

---

## Rule Set

### Dealing Procedure

| Rule | Setting |
|------|---------|
| **Dealing style** | **ENHC** — European No Hole Card. The dealer receives only one card face-up at the start of the round. The dealer does **not** check for blackjack, and does not receive a second card until all player hands have been completed. |

> ⚠️ This is a critical distinction from American-style dealing. There is no peek for dealer blackjack.

---

### Blackjack Payout & Additional Bets

| Rule | Setting |
|------|---------|
| **Additional bets vs dealer blackjack** | **OBBO** — Original Bets Only. If the dealer completes their hand and reveals a blackjack, only the player's original bet is lost. Additional wagers placed during the round (doubles, splits) push (are returned) rather than being lost. |

> OBBO partially compensates players for the lack of a hole card peek under ENHC rules.

---

### Dealer Soft 17

| Rule | Setting |
|------|---------|
| **Soft 17** | **H17** — Dealer hits on soft 17 (Ace + 6 = 17). The dealer must continue drawing until reaching hard 17 or higher, or a soft total of 18 or higher. |

---

### Splitting & Doubling Rules

| Rule | Setting |
|------|---------|
| **Double After Split (DAS)** | ✅ **Allowed** — Players may double down on any hand resulting from a split. |
| **Doubling on soft totals** | ❌ **Not allowed** — Players may not double down on soft hands (any hand containing an Ace counted as 11). Doubling is restricted to hard totals only. |
| **Splitting Aces** | ⚠️ **Once only** — Aces may be split, but the resulting hands cannot be re-split. Each Ace receives exactly one additional card. No further action (hit, double) is permitted on split Ace hands. |

---

## Summary Cheat Sheet

```
ENHC        European No Hole Card — no peek, no early resolution
OBBO        Original Bets Only — doubles/splits push vs dealer BJ
H17         Dealer hits soft 17
DAS         Double After Split allowed
No soft DD  Cannot double on soft totals
Aces once   Split Aces receive one card each; no re-splitting
```

---

## Implementation Notes

- **Strategy engine**: Any basic strategy charts, deviation tables, or EV calculations generated by this project must account for the full rule set above — particularly ENHC + OBBO, which materially affects correct strategy vs a standard H17 game.
- **Split Aces**: After splitting Aces, the hand is considered complete once the single card is dealt. No hit, double, or further split logic should be applied.
- **Soft double guard**: Before executing any double-down action, validate that the hand is a hard total. Reject soft doubles at the rules layer, not just the UI layer.
- **OBBO enforcement**: When resolving a round where the dealer has blackjack, iterate over all player hands and return (push) any bet placed after the original wager. The original bet loses as normal.
- **ENHC flow**: Dealer hand resolution must occur **after** all player hands are settled. Do not implement a mid-round peek or early-blackjack shortcut.

## Out of Scope (Unless Explicitly Added)

- Insurance / Even Money (not offered under this rule set unless specified)
- Surrender (not part of this variant unless specified)
- Re-splitting Aces
- Doubling on soft totals
